Tradix - ultimate trading experience!

Objective

The objective of the game is to become the wealthiest player through buying properties and completing projects. The game ends when no more properties left to trade.

Properties

There are two types of properties in the game - construction sites and construction projects. Construction projects are the ones that can bring profit, but they need certain number of construction sites to be acomplished.

There are four types of construction sites: residential, commercial, industrial and public. Each needed in particular kind of construction projects.

Construction Projects

To acomplish a construction project you need to have required set of construction sites. The project takes the top sites from your stake, if you have more than required.

When acomplished, the project and associated construction sites will be removed from your stack and you will be compensated by the value specified for the project (+$... in the parentheses).

Controls

Shift or Trade buttonStart Trade
Left click on propertyMove property up in stack
Double click on the top propertyAcomplish the project
Alt+click or Z+click on a propertyPut the property on sale
EnterComplete the trade/reset stakes
